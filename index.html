Frequently Asked Questions

How does career guidance work?
Our career guidance process involves personality assessment, skill evaluation, and one-on-one mentoring sessions to help you discover the best career path suited to your interests and abilities.
Is the mentorship service free?
We offer both free and premium mentorship services. Basic career guidance is free, while specialized one-on-one mentoring sessions are available through our premium plans.
How long does it take to find the right career path?
The timeline varies for each individual. Some students find clarity within a few sessions, while others may need more time for exploration. Our mentors work at your pace to ensure you make informed decisions.
Do you provide career guidance for all streams?
Yes, we provide comprehensive career guidance for all major streams including Science (Math/Bio), Commerce, Arts, and emerging interdisciplinary fields.
